Why Should you use openRAP

Easy maintance and monitoring

The openRAP Ecosystem

This Actor would be essentially a consumer of Data, either educational, entertainment or any other category. The consumption of Data can happen over a Handheld device (Phone/Tablet) or through Desktop using browsers.

The Work of this actor is to Educate end-users about the availability of openRAP devices and things like how to start and use openRAP and other logistic details. Teacher/Volunteer may also sometimes refresh the content of openRAP.
